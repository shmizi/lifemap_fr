# LifeMap

> An AI-assisted long-term life navigation system.

LifeMap is a roadmap-based productivity and planning platform designed to reduce overwhelm and make long-term goals feel actionable.

Instead of dumping users into endless task lists, LifeMap connects:

* goals
* subgoals
* milestones
* daily tasks
* AI-assisted planning

into one adaptive system that helps users consistently move forward.

---

# The Core Idea

Most productivity apps fail because they optimize for:

* task collection
* fake productivity
* dopamine loops
* endless organization

LifeMap focuses on something different:

> “Small actions visibly move the future forward.”

The app is designed to help users:

* break massive ambitions into manageable systems
* understand what matters today
* adapt when plans change
* maintain momentum without burnout

---

# Core Philosophy

LifeMap is **not**:

* a generic todo app
* a Notion clone
* a chatbot wrapper
* a habit tracker
* a second-brain app

LifeMap is:

> a visual long-term roadmap engine powered by structured planning and invisible AI assistance.

---

# Product Structure

The app follows a strict hierarchy:

## 1. Main Goals

Large long-term ambitions.

Examples:

* Become an ML Engineer
* Study Abroad
* Learn German B2
* Build a Startup

---

## 2. Subgoals

Major systems supporting a goal.

Examples:

* DSA Preparation
* Research Internships
* Language Certification
* Portfolio Development

---

## 3. Milestones

Medium-term checkpoints.

Examples:

* Complete A2 German
* Finish Graph Algorithms
* Publish Portfolio Website

---

## 4. Tasks

Small actionable units.

Examples:

* Solve 3 Leetcode questions
* Email professor
* Practice Goethe listening

Users primarily interact with tasks while roadmaps provide context and direction.

---

# Key Features

## AI Roadmap Generation

Generate structured plans from high-level goals.

Example:

```txt
“I want to study robotics in Germany.”
```

LifeMap converts that into:

* requirements
* milestones
* dependencies
* timelines
* actionable tasks

---

## Adaptive Replanning

Life changes.

Deadlines shift.
Goals evolve.
Burnout happens.

LifeMap recalculates roadmaps dynamically instead of forcing rigid schedules.

---

## Priority Intelligence

AI ranks tasks based on:

* urgency
* dependencies
* long-term impact
* roadmap health

---

## Momentum-Based UX

Instead of fake XP systems, LifeMap visualizes:

* consistency
* progress
* roadmap growth
* milestone completion
* future impact

---

## Layered Roadmap Visualization

### Layer 1 — Simple View

Minimal and calming.

### Layer 2 — Expanded View

Milestones and dependencies.

### Layer 3 — Graph View

Interactive roadmap visualization.

Complexity appears only when needed.
